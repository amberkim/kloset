What is Kloset?

When your child is growing quickly, what do you with their old clothes?

Kloset helps you find parents you can send those clothes to. Then you can
get clothes for the next season from other parents.

Give away your child's old clothes
Get new clothes for the next season

Step 1:
Give a box

Put a whole season's worth of clothes into one box and post pictures on the app.

Step 2:
Receive Koins

Koins are the in-app currency that will allow you to get other boxes of clothes. You can get Koins by putting boxes of clothes up or simply by purchasing them.
All funds from purchases will go to non-profits.

Step 3:
Get another box of clothes for free!
