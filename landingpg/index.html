The clothing exchange app for parents

Give away old clothes,
Get clothes for the next season

When your child is growing quickly, what do you with their old clothes?

Kloset helps you easily give away those clothes to other parents looking for that size.
Then you can get quality clothes for the next season from other parents. All for free!

Give a Box

Box up your child's clothes and post images and details about what's inside on Kloset. You'll receive a notice when someone requests your box and then you can contact them to find a time to give them your box.

Get a Box

Browse from all available boxes of clothes near you. Request your favorite boxes and simply pick it up at an agreed time or have it shipped to you.

Use Koins

Every time you post a box of clothes on Kloset, you receive Koins. Koins are used to get boxes. If you don't have any clothes to give away, you can also purchase Koins.

Be Aware of Your Impact

Now you'll know your child's outgrown clothes will actually go to someone who needs and wants it instead of donating it randomly. In return, you can also receive clothes from other parents for free.
